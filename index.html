PROJECT 1

MFANTSIPIM APP

The Mfantsipim Computer & Robotics Club Mfantsipim App is a comprehensive platform designed to keep the Old Boys of Mfantsipim School connected, informed, and engaged. It features real-time messaging for dynamic conversations and group discussions, fostering networking and reconnecting among the Old Boys. The app also provides easy access to academic records, allowing alumni to review their past achievements and current students to track their performance. Additionally, the app offers valuable admission information, including deadlines and requirements for prospective students. The event calendar keeps alumni updated on school events, reunions, and activities. Overall, the app strengthens the bond between the Old Boys and the school, serving as a vital tool for communication, information sharing, and community engagement.

PROJECT 2

M.V.P Bot

The Mfantsipim Computer & Robotics Club built an autonomous robot using the LEGO Mindstorms. The robot is designed to move, navigate obstacles, and interact with its environment using motors and sensors. It can travel forward, backward, and turn, while also avoiding obstacles with proximity sensors. The robot is programmed to perform tasks like line-following and object detection, reacting in real-time to its surroundings. Using block-based coding, club members applied engineering and programming skills to bring the robot to life, showcasing their ability to solve problems and work with advanced robotics technology. This project highlights the club’s innovation and commitment to learning in the field of robotics.

PROJECT 3

MFANTSIPIM A.I

The Mfantsipim Computer & Robotics Club developed an innovative AI-based system designed to assist the facilitators in various tasks such as; answering frequently asked questions in class, providing real-time assistance. The AI, powered by machine learning algorithms, is capable of understanding natural language input and responding with relevant information. It was trained to handle a range of school-related queries, such as details about schedules, events, admission processes, and general school information. The AI was integrated into a user-friendly interface, allowing students, staff, and visitors to easily interact with it via text. This project demonstrates the club's ability to apply AI and machine learning concepts in a practical setting, showcasing their skills in creating intelligent systems that can enhance the efficiency and experience of the school community.

PROJECT 4

3D MODEL OF THE SCHOOL STADIUM

For our Speech Day project, we created a detailed 3D model of the school stadium to better visualize the layout and functionality of this important space, which is used for sports events, school assemblies, and various activities. Using specialized 3D modeling software, we carefully recreated the stadium's structure, including the seating arrangement, the sports field, and other significant features such as entrances and facilities. This model provided us with an opportunity to explore various design improvements, such as optimizing seating for better views and ensuring the stadium is accessible to everyone. Through this project, we not only honed our technical skills but also learned the value of collaboration and creative problem-solving. Working together, we developed a deeper understanding of both the stadium’s design and the process of creating digital models, all while imagining how the space could be enhanced or adapted in the future.

PROJECT 5

MFANTSIPIM TRIVIA APP

For our Speech Day project, we developed a School Trivia App designed to engage students by testing their knowledge of the school’s history, traditions, and key events. The app makes learning interactive and fun, fostering curiosity and school pride in a game-like format. With a user-friendly interface and interactive features, the app appeals to both new and seasoned students, offering a fun way to explore our school’s culture. This project allowed us to combine creativity and technical skills while promoting teamwork and innovation. The app encourages learning, exploration, and community building, showing how technology can enhance education and bring students closer to what makes our school unique.
